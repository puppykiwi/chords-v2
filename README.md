The long awaited, by a handful of people, successor to [Chords v1](https://github.com/puppykiwi/chords) is finally here! With modern themes and a more secure 0auth

## ‚ú® Features

* **OAuth 2.0 Authentication:** Secure login flow that persists your session (no need to log in every time).
* **Playlist Browser:** View and navigate your saved Spotify playlists in a dedicated sidebar.
* **Track Viewer:** Browse songs within playlists with details like Title, Artist, and Album.
* **Remote Control:** Acts as a controller for your active Spotify device (Desktop, Mobile, or Web Player).
* **Real-time Playback:** Shows the currently playing song and a live progress bar.
* **Keyboard Shortcuts:** Control playback without touching your mouse.
